Verify Contact Information

This is a password-less application you can use to update your contact information for the LFA. We would like a non-Langara email address and mobile phone number to reach you in the unlikely event that members are unable to access the employer's email system.

There are 4 steps to the procedure which you should be able to complete in less than 10 minutes. You will need access to your Langara email, your personal email, and your mobile phone.

  1. The first step is to click on the "Get started!" button below. You will be asked to verify your Langara email address.
  2. You will receive a one-time link (good for 1 hour) in your Langara email. If you follow the link, you will be shown your current non-Langara email address and mobile phone number that the LFA has on file for you. If it is up to date, no further action is required. Otherwise, update the information and submit the form.
  3. If you chose to update your non-Langara email address, you will receive a one-time link at that email address. Follow that link to complete the update.
  4. If you chose to update your mobile phone number, you will receive a text message on your phone. Reply with 1 to complete the update.
